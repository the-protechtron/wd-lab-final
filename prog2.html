University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering (UVCE), Bengaluru

Infrastructure Overview

Building Details
Main Block Houses administrative offices, library, and lecture halls.
Computer Science Block Equipped with modern labs, classrooms, and faculty offices.
Mechanical Block Includes workshops, labs, and lecture rooms for mechanical engineering students.
Electrical Block Contains electrical labs, classrooms, and faculty offices.
Hostel Facilities Separate hostels for boys and girls with mess and recreational facilities.
Sports Complex Includes a gymnasium, basketball courts, and a cricket field.
Auditorium Large auditorium used for events, seminars, and conferences.
Library A vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources.

Additional Facilities

Facility Details
Wi-Fi Campus High-speed internet access available throughout the campus.

Parking Ample parking space for students, faculty, and visitors.
